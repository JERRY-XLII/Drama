[Youtube] Youtuber makes a call-out "expose" video about popular gamer "glitch video" uploader.

7  2012-12-19 by TwasIWhoShotJR

JamiexElite is a very popular "glitch video" uploader who, well, uploads videos of video game glitches. He has amassed over 240,000 subscribers, and all of his vids get views in the thousands.

Well, Youtuber ChaoticRavenger thinks JamexElite is a, " Liar, Scammer, Botter, Money Whore," who buys his subscribers off of eBay. He believes this so deeply that he made this lovely, video calling him out on it.

The veracity of his accusations is pretty secondary to the fact that, JamiexElite made a video in reply.. Jamie denies all allegations, calls all of Chaotic's "proof" completely fake, and goes as far as saying Chaotic made a fake eBay.

Since this video was posted only 2 hours ago, this drama is officially on-going, and any updates will be posted here.
