[Meta] We have grown, and have made some changes.

26  2012-12-29 by TwasIWhoShotJR

We have grown from 100 subscribers to 1600 in a very short period of time! Horray! As we have accumulated more drama junkies, the mods here have decided to grow with ya'll and make some changes to our moderation style (it won't hurt, I promise).

There are some very minor rule changes to our sub, mostly concerning trolls.

Trolls will be banned. We don't need trolls in here fucking our discourse up with their boring everything. If you are here to simply troll, either post a funny [Rumor] post, or get out :).

Users of excessive slurs - not cute. If the only shit they can talk consists of mindless slurrage, then they really don't belong here - or anywhere really (how they reconcile that fact, we don't care).

The mods will be handing out warnings before any ban, and acting all super rude about a warning will probably land you a ban. So just edit your offending post and you won't be banned. It's literally that easy.

This does not mean /r/Drama is a "safe space," or a social justicey preschool where all bad words are hanged upon. It means that if you want to get your point across, use real words. Actual sentence structure and other fancy literary shit is far more entertaining to read than low-brow 4chan tweenaged garbage.

So, basically, be a bitch, and not a dick. It's not super hard to figure out, and if you have any questions about our new rules, please feel free to ask.

EDIT: I forgot to mention!

If all you can talk about is SRSsucks, Anti-SRS, or SRS, we will so make fun of you for not having a life, or for having anything interesting to say, so keep that Reddit-centric political bullshit out of our sub. Thanks.
