Starbucks makes a commitment to the UK in a blog post. Leaves comments on. Whoops.

2  2012-11-13 by jecniencikn

http://starbucks.co.uk/blog/starbucks-commitment-to-the-uk/1240

There's a big kerfuffle in the UK at the moment about the amount of corporation tax paid by some companies. Starbucks is one of those companies. They successfully operate in the UK, and make a profit. But because of bizarre accounting they report no profit in the UK, and thus avoid (legally) paying corporation tax.

They got called before parliament to discuss this.

They then make a blog post to "share the facts with you on this important issue."

They left comments on. The comments are full of people who see through the transparently deceptive lies that Starbucks claims.

It's quite delicious, and deserves to be shared around.
